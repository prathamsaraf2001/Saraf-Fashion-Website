Products

We have a World-Class Infrastructure which facilitates our top quality production.

To start with, our Head Office is contemporary & elegant, with a fully equipped Showroom having an exclusive collection of our products which assists our Customers to get what they want..
The Greige Fabrics we purchase is manufactured on Auto-Looms, Sulzers, Air-Jet & Water-Jet Looms with the Finest Quality Yarn available in the market.
Processing of the Greige Fabric is carried out in Top-Quality & Contemporary Process Houses of Mumbai & Ahmedabad. Thus, providing us with the Finest Quality Textile Products time & again.

Greige Fabric

We deal in all types of Woven & Knitted Fabrics from Tirupur (T.N.), Erode (T.N.), Bhiwandi (Maharashtra) & Surat (Gujarat) in all Counts & Constructions. We assure you the best price & delivery for Greige Fabric.

Processed Fabric

We Dye & Print all types of Fabrics in Power Process Houses in Mumbai & Ahmedabad on Job-Work basis. We specialize in Pigment Dyeing. We have over 5000 Print Designs for our customers & we strictly maintain & the highest Quality & Sharpness in Printing.
