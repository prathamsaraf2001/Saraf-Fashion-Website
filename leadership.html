The Team

Driving innovation with a focus on topline and profitable growth in core business

Saraf Fashion is a diversified group with majority business interests in Textile & Apparel sectors.

Having enjoyed the patronage of many consumers, Saraf Fashion is reckoned for delivering world class quality products to its consumers since the past nine decades.

Our Vision is to create & offer our customers with Finest Quality products which meet International Standards & Customer Specifications, thus, ensuring Customer Satisfaction at all times. Our Group is committed at spreading its wings world over to novel trading endeavors, to cater to even a larger Clientele base.
